Cheese Burger

A cheeseburger is a hamburger topped with cheese. Traditionally, the slice of cheese is placed on top of the meat patty. The cheese is usually added to the cooking hamburger patty shortly before serving, which allows the cheese to melt.

Double Patty Burger

Double meat means the cook takes two portions of same size and smushes them together. This practice goes back to the beginning of the hamburger—earlier than White Castle even. In the interest of speed and timeliness, places would make small burgers, and they'd portion the beef.

Drinks

Lemonade

Lemonade is a sweetened lemon-flavored beverage. There are varieties of lemonade found throughout the world. In North America and South Asia, cloudy still lemonade is the most common variety.

Iced Tea

Iced tea is a form of cold tea. Though it is usually served in a glass with ice, it can refer to any tea that has been chilled or cooled. It may be sweetened with sugar or syrup.

Soda

A soft drink is a drink that usually contains water, a sweetener, and a natural and/or artificial flavoring. The sweetener may be a sugar, high-fructose corn syrup, fruit juice, a sugar substitute, or some combination of these. Soft drinks may also contain caffeine, colorings, preservatives, and/or other ingredients.

Milk Tea

To fill in the gap, they developed a drink that we can be considered a snack and beverage simultaneously, the milk tea. Milk tea is a refreshing flavored iced tea with tapioca balls at the bottom. Milk is mixed with a tea base, fruits, and flavored syrups, and later, the tapioca balls are added.
